Frequently Asked Questions

What is EduVault?

EduVault is a comprehensive resource platform designed specifically for college students. We provide organized study materials, notes, previous year papers, and other educational resources categorized by branch, semester, and subject.

How do I navigate through resources?

Simply select your branch (e.g., Computer Science), then choose your semester, and finally the subject you need resources for. All materials are organized in an easy-to-navigate structure.

Are all resources free to access?

Yes, all educational resources on EduVault are completely free to access and download. Our goal is to make quality educational materials accessible to everyone.

Can I contribute my own study materials?

Absolutely! We encourage students to contribute their notes, summaries, and other helpful materials. Please use the Contact page to submit your resources or suggestions.

Which branches are currently supported?

Currently, we support Computer Science (CSE) and Computer Science & ML (CSM) branches. We're continuously working to expand our coverage to include more branches.

How often are resources updated?

Resources are updated regularly, particularly at the beginning and end of each semester. We also update content whenever new curriculum changes are announced or when better materials become available.
